there are a few special effects included in this engine
1. screenshake
to use this simply do:
screenshake(_time, _magnitude, _fade)
more information on these arguments are included in the script
2. color inversion
startColorInversion() / stopColorInversion()
hopefully self explanatory
3. screen rotation
global.screen.LerpToAngle(newAngle)
or
global.screen.SetAngle(newAngle)
